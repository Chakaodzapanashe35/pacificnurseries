Seasonal Care Tips

Learn how to care for your plants through the changing seasons.

Read More

In spring, focus on fertilizing your plants as they start to grow.
In summer, ensure that your plants are adequately watered.
In fall, prepare for dormancy by reducing water and fertilizer.
In winter, protect your plants from frost and extreme temperatures.

Common Plant Diseases

Identify and treat common diseases that affect houseplants.

Read More

Common diseases include:
- **Powdery Mildew**: White powdery spots can be treated with fungicides.
- **Root Rot**: Caused by overwatering; ensure proper drainage.
- **Leaf Spot**: Remove affected leaves and improve air circulation.
